Bio

Marinos Kontos, otherwise artistically named as ''EKPE'',' (ekpe pronounciation), is a musician who lives and operates in Athens and is a member of the rap group Pixel. He is an artist whose musical influences range from Rap - Trap to Pop and R&B. His music has strong elements of melody, meaning and authenticity, as he says: "being authentic is the best marketing".

He has been involved in music since 2013 and during these years has attended vocal and music theory courses for three years, has managed to release over 20 tracks on youtube and on related platforms, which have gathered over 100,000 views. Ekpe has also appeared in stages in Athens and Tripoli, to hold entertainment shows and concerts.

His music affects people from all over the country and continues to inspire more and more people. In May 2020, his debut Album-EP entitled "America" is released, taking a tour of the thoughts and realizations he made during his recent trip to ‘’the land of opportunity’’. Finally, we could say that we are talking about an upcoming artist· Or maybe the next big thing...
